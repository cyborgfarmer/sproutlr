FOOD + JUSTICE

There's another way to eat.

Sprout Urban Farms Co. (SUFCo) is a cooperative network of growers, herbalists, nutritionists, beekeepers, and entrepreneurs working together to produce more local goods and build smart urban farms in Little Rock.

A local economy means more small businesses, better-paying jobs, healthier and more environmentally-friendly options for consumers, and a more creative and empowered populace. We’re bringing people together to build it.

SUFCo prizes collaboration and believes in equal pay for equal work, because there’s no CEO, just farmers and makers working together toward one purpose: a thriving local economy. But this isn’t just any local economy. This one’s ours, and that means all of us. We’re dreaming up and experimenting with new models of payment, such as pay-what-you-can and subsidizing food items with high-value specialty crops.

We’re also looking for ways to blend ecological principles with smart technology on the farms we partner with to reduce workload, maximize production, and increase efficiency when it comes to using limited resources like water.

The food landscape in Little Rock is changing, and a lot is going to change with it.

OUR MISSION

HIGH-QUALITY, LOW COST

We work with local farmers in growing high-value specialty crops and build partnerships with value-added producers and sellers to subsidize food costs. We’re experimenting with some pretty neat models, like pay-what-you-can for produce.

TECHNOLOGY + INNOVATION

We strive to blend ecological principles—such as companion planting and chemical-free growing—with advanced farm technologies—such as smart irrigation controllers and plant sensors—to make life easier for growers and bolster production.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Wages are better when businesses are cooperatively owned and production is local. Food systems are an integral part of life, and they should be owned and operated by their true stakeholder: the community.

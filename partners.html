OUR PARTNERS

Sprout Urban Farms Co. (SUFCo) is a cooperative network of growers, herbalists, nutritionists, beekeepers, and entrepreneurs working together to produce more local goods and build smart urban farms in Little Rock. A local economy means more small businesses, better-paying jobs, healthier and more environmentally-friendly options for consumers, and a more creative and empowered populace. We’re bringing people together to build it. SUFCo prizes collaboration and believes in equal pay for equal work, because there’s no CEO, just farmers and makers working together toward one purpose: a thriving local economy. But this isn’t just any local economy. It's ours, and that means all of us.
